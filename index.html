Podcast's series:

Geophony: Nature's Raw Sounds

Uncover the hidden soundtrack of the natural world. This podcast explores the raw, wild soundscapes created by wind, rustling leaves, and the ever-shifting elements. Each episode reveals a new sonic tapestry, inviting you to slow down, listen deeply, and connect with the primal rhythms of nature.

Anthrophony: Street Ambient Sounds

Embark on a sonic journey through the bustling streets. This podcast is your ticket to urban adventures without leaving your seat. Each episode features authentic street ambiences from diverse locations, inviting you to tune in, unwind, and find focus amidst the vibrant soundscapes of the city.

Biophony: Nature's Sonic Textures

Discover the hidden rhythms and melodies of nature with this podcast. Dive into the lush soundscapes of birds, animals, and insects in their vibrant habitats. Explore intricate auditory textures and immerse yourself in the unique symphony of natural sounds, enhancing your sensory experience.
